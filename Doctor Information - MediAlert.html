Dr. Sarah Johnson

Cardiology Specialist

Experience: 15 years
Education: Harvard Medical School
Board Certified: American Board of Cardiology

Dr. Johnson is a leading specialist in cardiovascular health with over 15 years of experience. She specializes in preventive cardiology, heart rhythm disorders, and cardiac rehabilitation. Dr. Johnson takes a holistic approach to patient care, focusing on lifestyle modifications alongside medical interventions. She has published numerous research papers on heart disease prevention and is actively involved in clinical trials for innovative cardiac treatments.
